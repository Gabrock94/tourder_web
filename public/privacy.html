Information I Collect

Would it be for me, i’d collect zero informations. However, the way the service works, is as follow. First, your location is retrived via yuor device GPS. The location, expressed in coordinates, is then converted to a location expressed as city name, geocoding package (https://pub.dev/packages/geocoding), which in turn is based on Android’s location reversed geocoding (https://developer.android.com/reference/android/location/Geocoder). This is the only stage in which your exact location (coordinates) is used. Subsequently, the location (city name) is passed to Open Street Map (https://www.openstreetmap.org/) via the Overpass API (https://www.overpass-api.de/). This is done to obtain a list of points of interest (POIs) around you. The two services as access to your location expressed as city name, not your precise location (coordinates). Finally, each POIs is searched on Wikipedia via the Wikipedia API (https://en.wikipedia.org/api/), from which images and information about the POIs are obtained. As such, this services may have access to your location, as well as IP address and device’s information.
